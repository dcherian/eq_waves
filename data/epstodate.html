Two integer times in NetCDF files can be easily converted to calendar dates

The double-integer times in TAO/TRITON, PIRATA, and RAMA netcdf files are Julian Day (time1) and milliseconds since 00:00 GMT (time2). You can enter a double-integer time below to convert it to a calendar date. If you wish to do this in a program, see the example program which comes with your data (read_netcdf.f).

NOTE: the Julian Day values in time1 are counted from the day January 1 in the year -4713. You can see this if you enter 0 0 for time1 and time2 in the boxes below.
